About Us

The Taj Hotels are globally celebrated for their uncompromising standards of luxury, service, and hospitality, blending world-class amenities with the warmth of Indian tradition. Every guest at a Taj property is welcomed with signature rituals like aarti, tika, and garlands, reflecting India’s age-old custom of honoring visitors. The rooms are designed to offer supreme comfort, featuring high-thread-count linen, a personalized pillow menu, and premium bath amenities. Many properties provide dedicated butler service and round-the-clock concierge assistance, ensuring that every guest’s needs are anticipated and fulfilled with thoughtful attention to detail. Taj hotels also uphold impeccable dining standards, with multiple specialty restaurants offering authentic regional and international cuisines prepared using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. The brand is particularly known for its afternoon high teas and curated heritage dining experiences, celebrating India’s rich culinary history. With personalized service, luxurious facilities, and a deep respect for cultural traditions, Taj Hotels set the benchmark for hospitality not just in India, but around the world.Taj Hotels, a prestigious name under the Indian Hotels Company Limited (IHCL), is synonymous with luxury, refined service, and timeless Indian hospitality. For over a century, Taj has set the gold standard in the hotel industry by blending modern amenities with the rich heritage and culture of India. From the moment a guest arrives, they are greeted with traditional customs like an aarti, tika, and flower garland, symbolizing warmth and respect. The properties are meticulously designed to reflect both grandeur and comfort, offering lavish accommodations equipped with high-thread-count linens, luxurious pillow menus, premium bath amenities, and state-of-the-art technology. Every detail is carefully curated, from monogrammed pillowcases for regular guests to customized room setups based on personal preferences. Taj’s legendary butler service and 24-hour concierge ensure personalized attention, catering to everything from travel itineraries and spa appointments to bespoke dining experiences. Culinary excellence is a defining aspect of Taj Hotels, with specialty restaurants that celebrate diverse international flavors and India’s rich culinary legacy. Menus are crafted with locally sourced, fresh ingredients, offering guests vegan, gluten-free, Jain, and other custom options on request. Signature experiences like heritage walks, high teas, and historic dining at landmark properties such as The Taj Mahal Palace Mumbai and Taj Lake Palace Udaipur, transport guests to a bygone era of regal elegance. Beyond material luxury, it’s the heartfelt service, attention to cultural detail, and commitment to guest delight that have earned Taj its enduring reputation as one of the finest hotel brands in the world.
